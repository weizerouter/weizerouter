# Packages & Pricing Tiers


WeizeRouter offers flexible plans tailored to developers and production applications.


## Plans

- **Models**: wz/grok-3 only

- **Rate Limit**: 10 RPM


### Uji Coba
- **Availability**: 1 day access
- **Models**: wz/grok-3 only
- **Price**: Rp2.000

### Pro

### Ultimate
